Frequently Asked Questions

Find all the essential information you need in our FAQ section, designed to address the most frequently asked questions and help you make informed decisions for your child's education.

What are the school hours at Little Learners Academy?

−

Our school hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday to Friday. We also offer extended care options for parents who need early drop-off or late pick-up.

How do you handle food allergies and dietary restrictions?

+

We carefully track every child's allergies and dietary needs and work with parents to ensure safe meal planning for everyone.

Is there a uniform policy for students?

+

Yes, students are required to wear a simple school uniform during regular school days. Information about where to purchase it is provided upon enrollment.

What is the teacher-to-student ratio at Little Learners Academy?

+

Our average teacher-to-student ratio is kept low so every child gets enough personal attention during the day.

What extracurricular activities are available for students?

+

We offer art, music, sports, and various clubs after the main school hours so kids can explore their interests.

How do you handle discipline and behavior management?

+

We use a positive, supportive approach focused on guiding children and helping them understand the impact of their actions.

How do I apply for admission to Little Learners Academy?

+

You can apply by visiting the Admissions page, filling out the enrollment form, and our staff will contact you with next steps.
